Amenities at Mariden Resort Siargao

Everything you need for a relaxing island stay, just 5 minutes from Siargao Airport (IAO).

The Pool: Your Oasis

Our swimming pool is the heart of the resort. It's the perfect place to cool off after a day of exploring or just to float around with a good book. We keep it clean and inviting, so you can jump in whenever you feel like it. It's your own little piece of paradise.

Pool Hours: Open daily from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM (closed during monthly maintenance)

Your place for your next event

Cottage ₱1,500.00

Our largest cottage is built for grand gatherings, accommodating 50-60 guests. Whether you're hosting a birthday party, a company outing, or a big family reunion, this is the perfect venue to celebrate in comfort and style.

Cottage ₱1,000.00

Designed for larger groups, this spacious cottage is great for reunions and small celebrations, with plenty of room for 20-30 guests. Enjoy a festive atmosphere with friends and family, making memories that will last a lifetime.

Cottage ₱500.00

Perfect for intimate family gatherings or a small group of friends, this cozy cottage comfortably accommodates 10-15 guests. It's an ideal spot to relax, share a meal, and enjoy quality time together in a peaceful setting.

Event Packages

Package A ₱4,000.00

This is the ultimate choice for your next party! Our Package A includes a spacious ₱1,000 cottage (perfect for 20-30 guests), so your entire group can celebrate together in comfort. As an added bonus, enjoy FREE videoke to sing your hearts out all day long, and the entrance is free for everyone.

Includes: ₱1,000 Cottage, FREE Videoke, Free Entrance for All Guests

Package B ₱5,000.00

Elevate your event with our premium package! Package B offers everything you need for a grand celebration, including a large ₱1,500 cottage that can host up to 50-60 guests. With FREE videoke and free entrance, this package is designed for big groups ready to have an unforgettable time.

Includes: ₱1,500 Cottage, FREE Videoke, Free Entrance for All Guests

Package C ₱10,000.00

Experience ultimate exclusivity with our premium resort rental! Package C grants you access to the entire resort for your private event. Perfect for weddings, corporate events, or grand celebrations where you want complete privacy and control of all facilities.

Includes: Entire Cottages Rental, FREE Videoke, Free Entrance for All Guests

Please note: If there are guests already checked in, they will be exempted from this exclusivity and will still have access to all facilities for reasonable use.

On-Site Mini Store (Sari-Sari Store)

Craving a snack or need a cold drink? Our on-site mini store has you covered! Stock up on snacks, junk food, biscuits, chocolates, and a variety of beverages without leaving the resort. Perfect for late-night cravings or quick refreshments by the pool.

Available: Chips, candies, instant noodles, soft drinks, water, juice, and other convenience store essentials. Ask our staff for availability and current stock!

Payment Options: We accept cash and digital payments. Just ask us about your preferred payment method and we'll check if we can accommodate it!

Travel Convenience & Services

Free Starlink WiFi

Stay connected with our complimentary high-speed Starlink WiFi throughout the resort. Perfect for digital nomads, remote workers, or staying in touch with loved ones back home. Fast, reliable internet even on this beautiful island paradise.

Long-Term Stays

Planning an extended stay in Siargao? We welcome digital nomads and long-term guests with special weekly and monthly rates. Long-term packages include:

  • ✓ Free water & electricity
  • ✓ Complimentary Starlink WiFi
  • ✓ Full kitchen access with cooking facilities
  • ✓ Flexible room cleaning schedule

Contact us for personalized long-term rates .

Airport Transfers

Let us know when you're arriving, and we can arrange a convenient pickup or drop-off at Siargao Airport (IAO), just 5 minutes away. Contact us to book your transfer .

Laundry Service

Travel light and stay fresh! We provide reliable on-site laundry service so you can pack light and stay fresh throughout your island adventure. Service is available at ₱60 per kilogram (minimum ₱300).

Motorbike Rentals

Looking to explore Siargao on your own? We offer motorbike rentals, with special discounts for rentals of three days or more, so you can uncover the island's hidden gems at your own pace.

Island Tours

We can connect you with trusted local guides for island hopping, land tours, and other Siargao adventures. Ask us for recommendations !

Catering Services

Make your event truly memorable with our custom catering services! We offer tailored food and beverage packages designed to fit your budget and preferences. Whether you're hosting a birthday celebration, reunion, or special occasion, let us take care of the menu while you focus on enjoying your event. Contact us to discuss your catering needs .

Solar Power & Backup Generator

We're powered by solar energy with a backup generator to ensure your comfort and connectivity 24/7. No need to worry about island brownouts - we keep the lights on and your devices charged so you can relax without interruption.

Peaceful Reading Area

Find a quiet corner and get lost in a story. We have comfortable spots where you can relax in peace.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Mariden Resort and Homestay Siargao ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our WiFi services, or stay at our resort.

By making a reservation, visiting our website, or entering our Resort premises, you acknowledge and consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number, mailing address)
  • Booking and reservation details
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party payment providers)
  • Guest preferences and special requests
  • Communication history with us

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referring website addresses
  • Analytics data through Google Analytics

2.3 CCTV and Video Surveillance

For the safety and security of all guests and staff, we operate closed-circuit television (CCTV) cameras in public areas of the Resort, including:

  • Pool area
  • Reception and common areas
  • Parking areas
  • Other public spaces

CCTV cameras are NOT installed in guest rooms, bathrooms, or other private areas. Recorded footage is retained for security purposes and may be used in the investigation of incidents, accidents, or as required by law. Footage retention periods comply with Philippine data privacy regulations.

2.4 Photography and Video Recording

We may take photographs or videos of the Resort premises and public areas for marketing, promotional, and social media purposes. You may be incidentally captured in such images or videos while in public areas of the Resort. If you do not wish to be photographed or recorded, please inform our staff at check-in.

2.5 WiFi Usage Data

When you use our complimentary WiFi service, we collect:

  • Device MAC address and device type
  • WiFi connection times and duration
  • Data usage and bandwidth consumption
  • Acceptance of WiFi Terms of Service

WiFi usage is subject to our separate WiFi Terms of Service presented via captive portal. We reserve the right to monitor network activity to ensure compliance with acceptable use policies and to protect our network infrastructure.

2.6 Behavioral and Interaction Analytics

To improve user experience and optimize our website performance, we collect comprehensive behavioral analytics data through Google Analytics 4, including:

  • Button and Link Clicks: All clickable elements including call-to-action (CTA) buttons, booking buttons, navigation menu links, footer links, and external links
  • FAQ Interactions: Questions clicked, FAQ categories viewed, and engagement patterns
  • Room Browsing: Room types viewed, "Book Now" button clicks, and room image carousel interactions
  • Contact Method Selection: WhatsApp, email, phone call, and messenger clicks across all pages
  • Social Media Engagement: Clicks on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, YouTube, TikTok, and other social media links
  • Gallery Interactions: Image lightbox views and gallery navigation patterns
  • Form Submissions: Contact form submissions, inquiry form completion status, and form abandonment data
  • Navigation Patterns: Page-to-page navigation flows, menu usage, and user journey mapping

Purpose of Behavioral Tracking: This comprehensive tracking allows us to understand how visitors interact with our website, identify areas for improvement, optimize conversion funnels, and provide a better user experience. All tracking is anonymous and does not collect personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it through forms or booking systems.

Third-Party Analytics Service: We use Google Analytics 4 for behavioral tracking. Google Analytics may set cookies on your device to enable this functionality. For more information about how Google uses data, visit Google's Privacy Policy .

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Process and manage your reservations
  • Communicate with you about your booking
  • Provide customer service and respond to inquiries
  • Send you promotional materials (with your consent)
  • Improve our services and website experience
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Ensure safety and security through CCTV monitoring of public areas
  • Investigate incidents or accidents using CCTV footage when necessary
  • Marketing and promotional activities using photographs/videos of Resort premises
  • Manage WiFi network usage, ensure fair access, and prevent abuse
  • Protect our network infrastructure from security threats and excessive usage

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist us with booking systems, payment processing, and analytics (e.g., BookingKu, Google Analytics)
  • Online Travel Agencies: When you book through platforms like Booking.com, Agoda, or Airbnb
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Improve website functionality
  • Track user interactions and behavioral analytics

6.1 Cookie Consent

We respect your privacy and give you control over cookies. When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner that allows you to accept or decline analytics cookies. Your choice is stored locally on your device for 365 days.

We use Google Consent Mode v2 , which means:

  • If you accept cookies: We collect full analytics data to improve your experience and understand how visitors use our site.
  • If you decline cookies: We still collect privacy-safe, cookieless data that cannot identify you personally. This helps us understand general trends while respecting your privacy.
  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function (e.g., security, booking system) are always active and don't require consent.

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting our site.

6.2 Types of Cookies We Use

Google Analytics Cookies:

  • _ga - Distinguishes unique users and expires after 2 years
  • _gid - Distinguishes unique users and expires after 24 hours
  • _gat - Used to throttle request rate and expires after 1 minute
  • _ga_<container-id> - Persists session state for Google Analytics 4 and expires after 2 years

Cloudflare Cookies:

  • __cflb - Used for load balancing and routing
  • __cf_bm - Bot management cookie for security
  • cf_clearance - Clearance cookie for verified visitors

Essential Cookies:

  • Session cookies for booking system functionality
  • Security cookies for CSRF protection

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your website experience and limit certain functionality such as the booking system. To opt out of Google Analytics tracking, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on .

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Specific retention periods include:

  • Booking and Guest Information: Retained for up to 7 years for accounting and tax purposes as required by Philippine law
  • CCTV Footage: Retained for 30-90 days unless required for investigation or legal purposes
  • WiFi Connection Logs: Retained for up to 6 months for network security and troubleshooting purposes
  • Marketing Photos/Videos: Retained for the duration of their use in marketing materials; you may request removal at any time
  • Email Communications: Retained for the duration of the business relationship plus 2 years

9. Your Privacy Rights

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173), you have the following rights:

  • Right to be Informed: You have the right to be informed about the collection and processing of your personal data
  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Erasure/Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data for direct marketing, including profiling
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service in a structured, commonly used format
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with the National Privacy Commission of the Philippines

Specific Rights Related to CCTV and Photography:

  • Request access to CCTV footage in which you appear (subject to verification and security considerations)
  • Opt out of marketing photography by informing staff at check-in
  • Request removal of your image from marketing materials (where technically feasible)

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@maridenresort.com or visit our front desk. We will respond to your request within 15 days as required by Philippine law.

11. Children's Privacy

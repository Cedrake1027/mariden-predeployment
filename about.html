Mariden Resort: Family-Run Siargao Homestay Since 2022

From our family to yours, a warm island welcome.

From Farmland to a Place of Rest

Not long ago, this land was just simple farmland. During the pandemic, times were tough for everyone. We saw an opportunity not just to build something for our family's future, but to create jobs and help our local community here in Del Carmen.

So, we started building. This resort is our family's retirement plan, but it's more than that. It's our way of sharing the peaceful, authentic side of Siargao with visitors like you. We poured our hearts into creating a place where you can truly disconnect from the noise and reconnect with what matters.

Locally Owned, Personally Cared For

As a locally owned and family-run resort, we take great pride in making sure you feel at home. We believe in privacy, quiet, and genuine care. When you stay with us, you're not just a guest; you're part of our story. Explore our comfortable rooms and discover our amenities to see what makes Mariden Resort special.

Planning a longer stay?

We welcome digital nomads and long-term guests with special rates that include free water, electricity, Starlink internet, and kitchen access. Contact us for details.

Our Sustainable Practices

Powered by the Sun

Our resort is partly powered by solar energy, helping reduce our carbon footprint while providing a reliable source of electricity during island brownouts.

Reducing Plastic Waste

We've replaced single-use shampoo and soap sachets with refillable pump dispensers in every room, cutting down on hundreds of plastic containers each year.

Preserving Local Nature

Our landscape features native plants that thrive naturally in Siargao's climate, creating shade, reducing water use, and supporting local biodiversity.

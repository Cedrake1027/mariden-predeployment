2. Safety Responsibilities

  • a. While using the swimming pool, guests are responsible for their own safety.

5. Non-consumable Items and Deposit

  • a. Guests are not allowed to take non-consumable items such as but not limited to towels, blankets, etc., from the Resort premises.

6. Noise and Disturbances

  • a. Guests are expected to maintain a noise level that does not disturb other guests.
  • b. Quiet hours are observed between 08:00 PM to 10:00 AM.

7. Check-in and Check-out

  • a. Check-in time is 2:00PM. Check-out time is 12:00 NOON.
  • b. Early check-ins and late check-outs are free and subject to availability.

8. Pets

  • a. Pets are not allowed in guest rooms or common areas. Service animals are an exception and must be declared upon reservation.

9. Smoking

  • a. Smoking is strictly prohibited inside all Resort buildings. Designated smoking areas are provided.

Mariden Resort Safety Guidelines and Rules

To ensure the safety and well-being of all guests at Mariden Resort, we kindly ask you to adhere to the following guidelines and rules:

1. Swimming Pool Safety

  • a. Guests are required to shower before entering the swimming pool.
  • b. Diving is strictly prohibited in all areas of the pool.
  • c. Running and rough play in the pool area are not allowed.

2. Lifeguard Hours

  • a. Lifeguards are on duty from [specified hours].
  • b. Outside of lifeguard hours, guests swim at their own risk.

3. Children's Pool

  • a. The children's pool is designated for guests aged 12 and under.
  • b. Parents or guardians must supervise their children at all times.

4. Emergency Procedures

  • a. Familiarize yourself with the location of emergency exits and evacuation routes.
  • b. In case of an emergency, follow the instructions provided by Resort staff.

5. First Aid

  • a. The Resort has a designated first aid station. Please report any injuries or emergencies to the nearest staff member.

6. Fire Safety

  • a. Smoking is strictly prohibited inside all Resort buildings. Designated smoking areas are provided.
  • b. Do not tamper with or disable smoke detectors.

7. Noise Control

  • a. Guests are requested to keep noise levels to a minimum during quiet hours [specified hours].
  • b. Use headphones when listening to music or watching videos in public areas.

8. Security Deposit

  • a. A security deposit is required upon check-in to cover potential damages or missing items.
  • b. Guests are responsible for their belongings. Use the provided safety deposit boxes for valuable items.

9. Non-consumable Items

  • a. Taking non-consumable items such as towels, blankets, etc., from the Resort premises is strictly prohibited.

10. Respect for Others

  • a. Treat fellow guests and Resort staff with courtesy and respect.
  • b. Any form of harassment or disruptive behavior will not be tolerated.

11. Pets

  • a. Pets are not allowed in guest rooms or common areas, except for registered service animals.
  • b. Guests with service animals must inform the Resort upon reservation.

12. Compliance with Rules

  • a. Failure to comply with these safety guidelines and rules may result in eviction from the Resort without a refund.

Final Acknowledgement:

Upon entering and staying on the premises, you acknowledge that you have read and understood these safety guidelines and rules. Your cooperation ensures a safe and enjoyable experience for all guests at Mariden Resort.

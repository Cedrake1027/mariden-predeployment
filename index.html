"We booked at Mariden Resort as a desperate attempt to seek immediate shelter when Cebu Pacific cancelled our flights due to the weather. The rooms are spacious, aircon is cold, hot water for shower is available, and they also have food. Wifi is relatively fast. After talking with the owner, we realized that MaRiden is a combination of the couple's names: Marites and Riden. When their neighbors lost their jobs during COVID, Marites and Riden decided to convert it into a resort to give some work for them. I really appreciate stories like these.

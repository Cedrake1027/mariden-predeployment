Mariden Resort Siargao FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers.

How far are you from Siargao Airport (IAO)?

We're very close! It's only about a 5-minute drive from the airport to our resort. Super convenient for catching flights. Learn more about arrivals and transfers . with us.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we do. Just let us know your flight details in advance, and we can arrange a pickup or drop-off for you (₱200 each way). It takes the stress out of travel.

Are there places to eat nearby?

Definitely. There are several small, local eateries (we call them 'carinderias') just a few minutes away. It's a great way to try authentic Filipino food.

What time is check-in and check-out?

Our standard check-in time is 2:00 PM, and check-out is at 12:00 PM (noon). Early check-in is free, subject to availability. If you need flexibility with check-out time, just ask us, and we'll see what we can do.

Is there WiFi?
